Help & Support

How to Use This Portal

  1. Login/Register: To access the portal, you need to log in using your credentials. If you are a new user, please register by providing your details in the registration form.
  2. Profile Update: Once logged in, go to "My Profile" to update your contact details, education, and professional information.
  3. Explore Events: Check out the "Events" section to stay updated on upcoming alumni meetups, webinars, and other activities. You can also register for events through this portal.
  4. Connect with Alumni: Use the "Connect" tab to search for fellow alumni, join groups, or participate in discussions. You can also find alumni mentors and job opportunities here.
  5. Feedback & Support: Use the "Help" page to send your feedback or contact us for support.
