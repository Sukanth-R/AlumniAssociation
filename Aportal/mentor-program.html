Alumni Mentor Program

The Alumni Mentor Program is a great initiative designed to enhance the network among alumni by allowing experienced professionals to mentor their peers and juniors. This program provides a platform for alumni to connect, share their expertise, and help others achieve their professional goals.

Participation in the Mentor Program is managed through our peer-to-peer platform. Mentees can reach out to potential mentors, and mentors have the option to accept or decline these requests based on their availability and interests. The program is designed to be flexible, allowing mentors and mentees to define the terms of their engagement, making it a tailored experience to fit individual needs.

How to Register as a Mentor

Sign in to the Alumni website, then visit the Mentorship Portal. Click on the button labeled "Be A Mentor".

  1. Fill out the registration form with your details.
  2. Submit the form to complete your registration.
  3. Wait for a confirmation email with further details.
